# Mean temperature raster for the world.

Mean temperature raster in degrees Celsius (multiplied by 100) for the
world in 10 arc min of resolution.

## Usage

``` r
temp
```

## Format

A PackedStatRaster object.

## Source

Data was modified from WorldClim (<https://worldclim.com/>, downloaded
05/2014).

Hijmans, R.J., S.E. Cameron, J.L. Parra, P.G. Jones and A. Jarvis, 2005.
Very high resolution interpolated climate surfaces for global land
areas. International Journal of Climatology 25: 1965-1978.
